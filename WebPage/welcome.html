Human-Agent Interation Study

I am a PhD student under the direction of Professor Erin Chiou in the Department of Computing, Informatics and Decision System Engineering at Arizona State University. I am conducting a research study to understand the reciprocal exchange relationship between human and agent. I am inviting your participation, which will involve 15 minutes of your time. You need to answer some general questions about your habits and, play a game provided by this website. You have the right not to answer any question, and to stop participation at any time. Your participation in this study is voluntary. If you choose not to participate or to withdraw from the study at any time, there will be no penalty. The result of your participation in this study will be used in reports, presentations, or publications to answer some open questions in the area of Human-Robot Interaction (HRI). There are no foreseeable risks or discomforts to your participation. You do not need to provide your name, or release any private information for this study as your responses will be anonymous.
