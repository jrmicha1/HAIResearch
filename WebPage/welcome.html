Human-Agent Interaction Study

I am a PhD student under the direction of Professor Erin Chiou in the
Department of Computing, Informatics and Decision System Engineering
at Arizona State University. I am conducting a research study to understand
the reciprocal exchange relationship between human and agent.

I am inviting your participation, which will involve 15 minutes of your time.
You need to answer some general questions about your habits and, play a game
provided by this website. You have the right not to answer any question,
and to stop participation at any time.

Your participation in this study is voluntary. If you choose
not to participate or to withdraw from the study at any time,
there will be no penalty.

The result of your participation in this study will be used in reports,
presentations, or publications to answer some open questions in the area of
Human-Agent Interaction (HAI). There are no foreseeable risks or
discomforts to your participation.

You do not need to provide your name, or release any private information
for this study as your responses will be anonymous.

If you have any questions concerning the research study,
please contact the research team at: (mehrdad@asu.edu).
If you have any questions about your rights as a subject / participant
in this research, or if you feel you have been placed at risk, you can
contact the Chair of the Human Subjects Institutional Review Board,
through the ASU Office of Research Integrity and Assurance at (480) 965-6788.
Please let me know if you do not wish to be part of the study.
